CRM Cross-sell Capability Roadmap

3-phase roadmap to scale Zurich GI × Life CRM from broader reach to intelligent personalization.
Phase 1 Jun – Sep

Expand GI ↔ Life Cross-sell Reach

Increase reachable cross-sell inventory by embedding Life / GI offers into all GI ↔ Life lead moments and completing retention and lapse-stage touchpoints.

Key Deliverables
  • Add cross-sell to all GI ↔ Life lead moments
  • Launch remaining retention and lapse initiatives
  • Expand TM follow-up lead generation model
Phase 2 Oct – Dec

Complete Retention & Lapse Stage Cross-sell

Complete all retention and lapse-stage cross-sell initiatives to ensure GI × Life coverage across the full customer lifecycle.

Key Deliverables
  • Launch Retention Discount cross-sell
  • Launch Multi-policy Program eDM
  • Launch OZCP Multi-policy Cross-sell
  • Launch Winback eDM
  • Launch Lifestage & Seasonal eDM
  • Complete retention and lapse journey coverage
Phase 3 2027+

AI-driven Next Best Offer

Build a more personalized CRM recommendation engine across GI, Life and multi-policy journeys.

Key Capabilities
  • More personalized “Just in Time” CRM journeys
  • Combine all possible customer data, including browsing records and personal life events
  • Personalized eDM content
  • Personalized OZCP and TM recommendations
